JAPAN

Japan has been one of the countries I really want to visit for sure. I hope to go some day with my friends!

Reasons To Go

  • I would like to visit Shibuya Crossing because I've seen it in a lot of pictures and Japanese shows. I also want to go to Osaka Castle because I heard the view of the castle is amazing in-person.

  • Some of the food I want to try are: Ramen, Takoyaki, and Sushi. While these foods are sold in America, Japan has various versions of these. The ingredients and how they prepare them differ by district.

  • I want to experience their culture and see how their fashion differs from America's.

If I find more locations to visit to, I would add them on to my bucket list for the trip.
