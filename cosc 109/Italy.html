ITALY

Italy is just one of the many European countries that I want to visit. For this website, I will be only talking about Italy for this page.

Reasons To Go

  • Out of all of the countries I've been to, this would be the first European country I've visited.

  • One of the places I would like to visit in Italy is an ancient site in Rome. It would be cool to see the Colosseum in-person, especially because it was heavily referenced in history, and old stories.

  • Another place I would like to visit is the Vatican City. While I know it's still inside of Rome, this is a different site within Rome I would like to visit. St. Peter's Bascilica is within the Vatican City, and I've been told it has amazing art pieces from scenes in the Bible in it.

  • One of the foods I would like to try in Italy is pizza. While we speak highly of pizza in places like Chicago and New York, going to a country that is known to have amazing pizza would be a great experience.
