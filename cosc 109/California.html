CALIFORNIA

The west coast, specifically California, has different weather and experiences compared to the east coast of America. I have heard about many things happening in California that interest me a lot.

Reasons To Go

  •  I want to go to California because I'm interested in how the dialect and mannerisms are over there compared to the East Coast.

  • Some of anime's and comic's biggest events are hosted in California, and I haven't been able to attend them since I've been on East Coast. I would love to attend Anime Expo and Comic Con.

  • I also want to try eating at some of the food chains that are West Coast exclusive like Jack in the Box.
